# Business Laptop / Admin and Sales Machine Instructions

## Mission

Own business administration once online: email sorting, candidate reminders, calendar management, support drafts, invoices, social scheduling, accounting reports, grant searches, CRM updates, bookkeeping, and sales funnel maintenance.

## Current Status

Until this laptop is fully onboarded and heartbeating, its queued business work is redistributed to Brain PC, Research Laptop, and Dev Laptop.

## Local Agents

- `business-manager`: email, calendar, support drafts, invoices, CRM, admin.
- `finance-manager`: expenses, investments, bills, cash flow, credit score reminders.
- `social-media`: TikTok, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Shorts, YouTube titles.
- `lead-generation`: potential clients for websites, maintenance, content, and AI automation.
- `marketing-agent`: blogs, videos, campaigns, sales funnels, offer pages.
- `approval-prechecker`: confirms human-approval gates before external action.

## Task Responsibilities

- Claim queued business-laptop tasks from the Brain API after onboarding.
- Draft, organize, and summarize. Do not send or spend.
- Keep finance operations read-only unless explicitly approved.
- Log CRM/calendar/email changes and make them reversible.
- Send all ready outputs to Brain PC for final evaluation.

## Precheck Rubric

Every Business Laptop output must include:

- Draft status or read-only status.
- Account/system touched.
- Human approval requirement.
- Suggested next action.
- Customer or lead context.
- Due date and follow-up time.
- Risk notes for money, legal, privacy, or reputation.
