# Autonomous Business OS

This system is designed to help Jayla research, create, validate, and operate approved online business pipelines from a $0 starting budget toward a $25,000/month target.

It does not guarantee revenue. It creates a disciplined, auditable operating system for finding demand, building offers, producing deliverables, and escalating sensitive actions.

## Human Approval

Jayla must approve:

- banking, payment destinations, loans, accounts, and transfers
- legal filings, tax filings, contracts, terms, privacy, and regulated claims
- paid advertising or spending above $0
- customer-facing sends until the workflow is explicitly approved
- public publishing of pricing claims, guarantees, legal/health/financial claims, or complaint responses
- destructive operations, permanent data deletion, credential changes, and security incidents

## 30-60 Day Flow

1. Days 1-3: research 20 problems, shortlist 5, select 1 narrow offer.
2. Days 4-7: landing page, sample deliverable, pricing test, outreach list.
3. Days 8-14: minimum business setup, intake, support scripts, QA checklist.
4. Days 15-21: pilot fulfillment and testimonial request drafts.
5. Days 22-30: automate proven intake, draft production, QA, delivery, and reports.
6. Days 31-45: repeatable acquisition through outreach, content, referrals, and partnerships.
7. Days 46-60: improve margins, add upsell/subscription, refine conversion and support.

## Brain Endpoints

- `POST /business-os/seed` creates or updates the zero-budget business launch pipelines.
- `GET /business-os` shows launch projects, task counts, approval matrix, and laptop setup packets.
- `POST /business-os/model-sprint` asks configured models to critique and improve the business pipeline.
- `GET /business-os/laptop-setup/{machine_id}` returns step-by-step CLI setup prompts for each laptop.

## Laptop Role Split

- Brain PC: approval, orchestration, model routing, finance audit, security, reporting, Phoenix voice.
- Dev Laptop: websites, apps, APIs, package build, QA, Netlify/deployment prep.
- Research Laptop: markets, grants, competitors, Reddit/GitHub/news/government research.
- Business Laptop: CRM, customer drafts, marketing, sales funnels, bookkeeping summaries, support drafts.

Until Business Laptop is fully online, Brain, Dev, and Research share business work through the task queue.
