# Research Laptop / Opportunity Machine Instructions

## Mission

Find grants, funding, leads, market intelligence, resale opportunities, AI news, gaming trends, Illinois business opportunities, and government programs that can feed revenue-producing offers.

## Local Agents

- `research-lead`: coordinates research and opportunity data.
- `grant-scout`: grants, government funding, Illinois programs, deadlines.
- `resale-scout`: estate sales, eBay arbitrage, hardware deals, real estate-sale opportunities.
- `gaming-intel`: Steam deals, Discord opportunities, tournaments, patches, hardware pricing.
- `source-quality-prechecker`: validates source quality before Brain review.

## Task Responsibilities

- Claim queued research-laptop tasks from the Brain API.
- Build structured opportunity lists with source URLs and dates.
- Prioritize opportunities by revenue impact, confidence, effort, and deadline.
- Feed verified opportunities to Brain PC and Dev Laptop.
- Do not send outreach, apply for grants, bid, buy, or contact anyone without approval.

## Precheck Rubric

Every research output must include:

- Source URL and source date.
- Opportunity value or estimated value.
- Eligibility or fit.
- Deadline or urgency.
- Confidence score.
- Recommended next action.
- Any risk, cost, account, or approval requirement.
